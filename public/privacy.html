Privacy Policy for PRIMS Practitioner Invoicing

Effective Date: 13 March 2025

1. Introduction

PRIMS Practitioner Invoicing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose information uploaded to our platform by practitioners using our services. By using PRIMS Practitioner Invoicing, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect and use information to facilitate the core functionalities of PRIMS Practitioner Invoicing. The types of information we handle include:

  • Company and Practitioner Information: Any details you upload related to your company or practice will be stored to the best of our ability, subject to platform limitations. This information is used exclusively for generating tax invoices, accounting reports, and other financial documents as specified in our Terms of Service.
  • Clinical Reports and Confidential Data: Information uploaded from your clinical practice, including reports, will only be used during your active session. This data will not be stored to ensure the confidentiality and privacy of your patients.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect stored practitioner and company information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.
  • Clinical reports and other confidential patient-related data are not retained beyond an active session, ensuring that no sensitive patient information is stored within our system.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We will not share, sell, or disclose any uploaded information to external parties for any purpose, including data aggregation or resale.
  • Information is only used for generating tax invoices, reports, and accounting software export files, as described in our Terms of Service.
  • Access to uploaded data is strictly limited to necessary system functions and is not accessible to third-party entities.

5. Compliance and User Responsibilities

  • Users are responsible for ensuring that the information uploaded complies with applicable laws and regulations regarding data privacy and confidentiality.
  • Practitioners should ensure that sensitive patient information is managed in compliance with professional and legal confidentiality obligations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any modifications will be posted on our website with the updated effective date. Your continued use of PRIMS Practitioner Invoicing signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
